Grading

Your final grade is based on the following:

  • Paper presentation (10%)
    • During the semester, each student will present a paper for 15 minutes (12 min presentation plus 3 min Q&A). If the slides are public or there are videos available online, it is okay to reuse the slides, but make sure you make a high-quality presentation.
    • Click here to reserve a presentation slot (you can fill the paper title later).
  • Paper review (5 * 6% = 30%)
    • Each student is required to write five paper reviews for papers presented in the course. Submit your paper reviews on Brightspace. Please read this guideline before you write a review.
  • Two assignments (20%) for theory (proofs) and practice (programming)
  • Final project (group of size 1, 2 or 3: 40%)
    • Click here to register your team with a tentative title, and submit your final report on Brightspace. If desired, teams can utilize office hours to get feedback on the project.
